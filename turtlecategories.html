Turtle Categories

Five of the seven turtle species can be seen in the shores of Sri Lanka

Green Turtle

The Green Turtle is the largest of the sea turtles and the only herbivore among the various species. Green turtles are named after the greenish color of their cartilage and fat rather than their shells

Hawksbill

Hawksbills are named for their narrow, pointed beak. They also have a distinctive pattern of overlapping scales on their shells that form a serrated-look on the edges.

Loggerhead

The Loggerhead Turtles get their name from their large heads, which house powerful jaw muscles that allow them to crush hard-shelled prey like clams and sea urchins.

Leatherback

Leatherback turtles are named for their shell, which is leather-like rather than hard, like other turtles.They are the largest sea turtle species and also one of the most migratory, crossing both the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
